# AEGIS — Demo Script
## 2-Minute Timestamped Screen Recording Walkthrough

**Total runtime:** ~2:30  
**Recommended resolution:** 1280×720 or higher  
**Tip:** Open AEGIS in a full-browser window, hide browser chrome where possible, and pre-run a scenario simulation before hitting record so the results panel is ready.

---

## 00:00 – 00:12 · Cold Open — System State Banner

**On screen:** Overview page loads. The hero banner occupies the full width.

> "This is AEGIS — the AI Energy Geopolitical Intelligence System — a mission-control dashboard for monitoring and simulating disruptions to India's crude oil supply chain."

- Point to the large **NOMINAL / ELEVATED / CRITICAL** state indicator
- Point to **Global Risk score**, **SPR Cover days**, and the **LAST SYNC** timestamp top-right
- Say: "Every metric auto-refreshes every five minutes, or you can trigger it manually."

---

## 00:12 – 00:22 · Manual Refresh

**Action:** Click the **REFRESH ALL** button (top-right header).

> "Clicking Refresh All invalidates every cached query simultaneously and fetches live data from the PostgreSQL backend."

- Watch the button spin and the timestamp update
- Note the crimson pulse dot that appears during the sync

---

## 00:22 – 00:40 · Overview — Module Cards

**On screen:** Five glass module cards with sparklines.

> "Each card summarises one AEGIS module: Risk Intelligence, Scenario Modeller, Procurement Orchestrator, Reserve Optimizer, and Digital Twin."

- Hover over each card to show the lift animation
- Point to the colored trend badge (INCREASING / STABLE / DECREASING)
- Point to the area sparkline charts underneath each metric value

**Action:** Click **RUN DISRUPTION SIM** button on the right side of the banner.

> "Running a full disruption simulation triggers a backend computation across all modules simultaneously."

- Wait for the toast notification confirming completion

---

## 00:40 – 01:00 · Risk Intelligence

**Action:** Click **RSK** in the left sidebar.

> "The Risk Intelligence module gives us a real-time threat assessment of the five critical shipping corridors India depends on."

- Point to the **radial gauge dials** — Strait of Hormuz at 72, Red Sea at 68
- Say: "Scores above 65 indicate HIGH risk; above 80 is CRITICAL."
- Scroll down to the **Supplier Vulnerability Matrix**
  - Point to Iraq, Saudi Arabia, UAE, Russia
  - Show the share bar and risk badge per supplier
- Point right to the **Live Intel Feed**
  - Show the severity badges (HIGH / CRITICAL) and the extracted-risk text blocks

**Action:** Click **REFRESH SIGNALS** top-right.

> "This triggers a live signal refresh from geopolitical data sources."

---

## 01:00 – 01:28 · Scenario Modeller + Video

**Action:** Click **SCN** in the sidebar.

> "The Scenario Modeller lets analysts simulate geopolitical shocks and see cascading impacts across the entire supply chain."

**Action:** Click **Strait of Hormuz Closure**.

> "Let's model a partial Hormuz closure — 40% of India's crude supply passes through this corridor."

- Show the severity slider appearing; drag it to **8**
- Click **EXECUTE SIMULATION**
- Wait for the four impact metric cards to populate (refinery run rate, fuel price delta, SPR drawdown, spot price)
- Show the two charts — refinery run rate line chart and fuel price delta area chart

**Action:** Click **GENERATE VIDEO**.

> "AEGIS includes a built-in supply-chain visualization engine that animates the disruption across all four response phases."

- The modal opens and the **live canvas animation starts immediately** — shipping corridor flow particles, node pulse on Hormuz, phase labels cycling
- Point to the four phase labels at the bottom: PRE-DISRUPTION → DISRUPTION EVENT → CASCADING IMPACT → SYSTEM RESPONSE
- Show the **Play / Pause** and **Restart** controls
- Click **FULLSCREEN** to demonstrate full-screen canvas
- (If browser supports recording) Click **EXPORT WEBM** → show the progress bar filling → video player appears with Download button
- Close the modal

---

## 01:28 – 01:50 · Procurement Orchestrator

**Action:** Click **PRC** in the sidebar.

> "When Hormuz closes, the Procurement Orchestrator surfaces alternative sourcing options ranked by an AI agent."

- Show the four ranked supplier cards: RECOMMENDED (green) / VIABLE (amber) / MARGINAL (orange) / AVOID (red)
- **Click** the top-ranked card to expand it
- Point to the four metric columns: Spot Price, Tanker Availability, Port Congestion, Grade Compatibility
- Point to the **AI Agent Reasoning** block — structured justification text generated by the backend orchestrator
- Scroll right to show the **Global Crude Sources** sidebar with live prices

---

## 01:50 – 02:10 · Reserve Optimizer

**Action:** Click **RES** in the sidebar.

> "The Reserve Optimizer tracks India's Strategic Petroleum Reserve and models drawdown strategies."

- Point to the **central radial SVG gauge** — current days of cover, fill percentage
- Show the **Drawdown Modeller** — drag the release-rate slider
- Watch the three quick-stats update in real time (Days Remaining, Cost Impact, Supply Bridge)
- Point to the **projected inventory lifespan area chart** — shows reserve level declining with supply gap overlay

---

## 02:10 – 02:30 · Digital Twin Map

**Action:** Click **DGT** in the sidebar.

> "The Digital Twin is a live topological map of every node and shipping corridor in India's crude supply chain."

- Point to the SVG world map — color-coded corridors, animated flow particles, pulsing nodes
- **Click the Strait of Hormuz node** (orange dot, center of map)
  - Info panel populates on the right with coordinates, capacity, utilization, risk level
  - Click **SIMULATE CLOSURE** — what-if result appears with affected volume, alternative routes, and recommended action
- **Click the Arabian Sea corridor** line
  - Info panel switches to corridor intelligence
  - Click **SIMULATE DELAY**
- Show the four-item color legend at the bottom of the map

> "Every element on this map is fully interactive — AEGIS gives decision-makers a live, queryable digital replica of India's entire energy import infrastructure."

---

## 02:30 · Close

> "AEGIS is a full-stack, production-ready intelligence platform — PostgreSQL-backed, contract-first API, auto-refreshing every five minutes, with six specialized dashboards and a built-in simulation engine. Thank you."

## Recording Tips

| Setting | Recommendation |
|---------|---------------|
| Viewport | 1280×720 minimum |
| Browser | Chrome or Edge (best MediaRecorder support) |
| Audio | Narrate live or add voiceover in post |
| Cursor | Use a cursor-highlight tool (e.g. Cursor Highlighter) |
| Transitions | 0.5s crossfade between sections |
| Pre-run | Execute a scenario simulation before recording so results are instant |
